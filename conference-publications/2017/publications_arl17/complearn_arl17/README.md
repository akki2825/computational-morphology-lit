# Computational Learning of Morphology

link: https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-linguistics-011516-034017

authors: John Goldsmith, Jackson Lee, Aris Xanthos

A survey of computational approaches to learning morphology, covering both supervised and unsupervised methods, with emphasis on the linguistic and cognitive motivations for different approaches. Published in the Annual Review of Linguistics.
